Major Summary of the files:

ccCompass/CourseCatalog.html: loads catalog on browser

ccCompass/CourseCatalog.js: onclick functions for buttons and collapsibles, window.onload to load courses

ccCompass/Course.js: collects data from MIT course catalog, cleans and stores into JS objects

ccCompass/Tree.js: functions for animating the tree diagram

ccCompass/Jobs.js: cleans and performs single iteration knm on job data

ccCompass/LinkedIn/JobsAnalysis.js: performs multiple iterations on scraped data from LinkedIn (20 jobs)

ccCompass/LinkedIn/knm.html: loads button that performs multiple knm iterations on scraped data
